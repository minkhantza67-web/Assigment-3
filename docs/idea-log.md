## Idea 1: RSU Campus Buddy

### Problem Area
Students and visitors often have difficulty locating buildings and facilities on campus.

### Target Users
Students, staff, and visitors.

### Current Alternative
Google Maps, asking friends, security guards, or university staff.

### Proposed IT Solution
An AI-powered campus assistant that recognizes campus locations from uploaded photos and provides navigation guidance.

### Possible Technology
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, AI Image Recognition, Cloud Database.

### Why This Is Suitable
The project can be developed as a web-based MVP and tested with real students during the semester.
