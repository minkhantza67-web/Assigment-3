| Idea 1 | | | |
Our team is building a Smart Campus Chatbot with Image Recognition for Rangsit University. Students upload a photo of any campus building (inside or outside) and instantly receive its name, services, facilities, clubs, and opening hours via chatbot.
**Problem:** Students struggle to find buildings, services, and clubs on campus.
**Target Users:** New and current RSU students, visitors, and parents.
**Technology:** Web app (HTML/CSS/JS) + image recognition AI + Python/Flask backend + JSON database.
**Why Suitable:** A working prototype (image upload → AI recognition → chatbot response) is achievable within one semester using a small set of sample buildings, expandable later. |
