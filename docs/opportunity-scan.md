
# Lab 02 Opportunity Scanning
## Ideas Reviewed from Lab 1
| Idea 1| Problem Area | Target User | Current Alternative | Possible IT Solution | Feasible Technology |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| RSUBUDDY | Difficulty identifying campus buildings and finding service information | New students,international students,visitors | Ask staff or search university website/social media |RSU Campus Buddy is a web-based smart campus assistant that uses image recognition and chatbot technology. Students can upload a photo of a campus building to instantly receive information about facilities, services, operating hours, student clubs, and campus regulations. The chatbot also answers campus-related questions and helps students navigate university resources more efficiently. | HTML/CSS/JS landing page with Google Form for feedback,Figma clickable prototype with Google Sheets validation dashboard |
| Student Roommate Matching & Housing Platform | Students searching for accommodation near campus face challenges in finding roommates who are compatible in terms of budget, lifestyle, study habits, cleanliness, sleep schedules, and personal preferences. Existing platforms focus mainly on room listings and provide limited support for evaluating roommate compatibility, often resulting in conflicts, dissatisfaction, and frequent relocation | University and college students, international students, exchange students, and freshmen seeking shared accommodation. | Facebook housing groups, rental websites, property portals, student forums, WhatsApp/Telegram groups, and personal referrals. | Student Roommate Matching & Housing Platform that combines room listings with a compatibility-based matching system. Students complete a profile and preference questionnaire, receive compatibility scores, view recommended roommates, communicate through in-app messaging, and browse verified room listings. Additional features may include student verification, roommate reviews, location-based search, favorite lists, reporting tools, and housing recommendations. | HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP/Laravel, MySQL, Google Maps API, Firebase Chat API (optional). |
| Student Second-Hand Marketplace Platform | Students frequently own textbooks, electronics, furniture, dormitory supplies, and other items that become unnecessary after a semester or academic year. Existing marketplaces are often cluttered with non-student users, making transactions less trustworthy, while campus-specific buying and selling opportunities remain fragmented across multiple social media platforms. | University and college students, graduating students, exchange students, and dormitory residents. | Facebook Marketplace, campus Facebook groups, online classified websites, bulletin boards, and peer-to-peer social media transactions. | Student Second-Hand Marketplace Platform designed exclusively for verified students. The system enables users to create listings, browse categorized products, negotiate through chat, save favorite items, rate sellers, and track transactions. Advanced features may include recommendation systems, smart search and filtering, student verification, fraud reporting, item condition scoring, price comparison, and campus-specific marketplace communities. | HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP/Laravel, MySQL, Cloudinary/Image Upload API, Firebase Chat API (optional). |
